Our 16 Fundamental Beliefs

Click on a belief to view its full description.

  1. The Scriptures Inspired

    The Bible is the inspired Word of God, a revelation from God to man. It is infallible and authoritative.

  2. The One True God

    There is one true God, revealed in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

  3. The Deity of the Lord Jesus Christ

    Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, born of a virgin, and fully divine.

  4. The Fall of Man

    Man was created good but fell through disobedience, incurring physical and spiritual death.

  5. The Salvation of Man

    Salvation is through repentance and faith in the atoning sacrifice of Jesus Christ.

  6. The Ordinances of the Church

    The two ordinances are water baptism by immersion and Holy Communion.

  7. The Baptism in the Holy Spirit

    The baptism in the Holy Spirit empowers believers for witnessing and service.

  8. The Initial Physical Evidence of the Baptism in the Holy Spirit

    The initial evidence is speaking in tongues as experienced in Acts 2:4.

  9. Sanctification

    Sanctification is an act of separation from evil and dedication to God.

  10. The Church and Its Mission

    The Church is the Body of Christ and has the mission of evangelism and worship.

  11. The Ministry

    A divinely called and scripturally ordained ministry leads the Church in worship and service.

  12. Divine Healing

    Divine healing is provided in the atonement and is available for all believers.

  13. The Blessed Hope

    The Blessed Hope is the rapture of the Church at Christ's coming.

  14. The Millennial Reign of Christ

    The second coming of Christ includes the millennial reign on earth.

  15. The Final Judgment

    The wicked will be judged and eternally punished in the lake of fire.

  16. The New Heavens and the New Earth

    We look forward to a new heaven and earth where righteousness dwells.
