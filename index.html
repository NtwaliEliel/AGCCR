Who We Are

We are a vibrant and Christ-centered community dedicated to worshiping God, growing in faith, and sharing His love with the world. As part of the global Assemblies of God Fellowship, we are committed to spreading the Gospel, nurturing discipleship, and empowering individuals to serve with purpose and compassion. At Calvary Church, we strive to be a beacon of hope, a family of believers united in prayer, and a source of transformation for our local and global community.
